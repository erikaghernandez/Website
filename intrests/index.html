Podcasts

One thing that I never thought I would really enjoy was podcasts. But to my surprise it turns out they are not that bad. Lately, I have been listening to The Last Podcast on the Left, which is under the categories of dark comedy and true crime. I also occasionally listen to Impaulsive which lies under the entertainment category. I have been wanting to expand my podcast library and see what else I like.

Stuff You Should Know: Short Stuff

In my quest to find more podcasts to listen to I ran across this particular podcast. At first I started to listen to the regular 45-50 minute podcast but I found myself getting too distracted. Then I noticed that there were also shorter podcasts under the same creators. They are referred to as "Short Stuff". They are shorter, just 15 minutes! Which I find quite nice because I can listen to one episode as background without necessarily focusing all my attention.

My top 3 Short Stuff episodes:

  1. Cramming
  2. 666
  3. Space Hurricanes

Yeah No, I'm Not Okay

"Growing up, I was taught to say that I was "ok" when I really wasn't."

This podcast was created by one of my favorite actors, Diane Guerrero. She is an actor with Latino heritage which is important because in this podcast she focuses on addressing mental health. But by no means does this podcast give clinical advice but it is rather an open conversation on the topic. I have just listened to one episode and so far I like it so I will definitely listen to a second episode.
